Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate in Environmental Economics at the London School of Economics and Political Science, with a research focus on technological change and climate policy.

My research primarily focuses on the economic benefits of green technological changes and their potential to foster technology spillovers. I also investigate the impact of climate policies on changes in firm behaviour, with a particular focus on China.
